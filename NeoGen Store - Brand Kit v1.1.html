01 / live store audit

What The Store Is Already Saying

Reviewed against the live homepage at https://neogen.store/ on 2026-04-22 and the four exported AI brand files in this repo.

Keep

The systems-console direction fits the catalog: networking, homelab, smart home, high-performance gaming, and services. It feels specialized instead of generic ecommerce.

Strengthen

The header lockup is correct in concept, but it is too small against the hero. The brand needs a stronger first-viewport signal without becoming noisy.

Clarify

The older exports let the NG monogram and 8-point star compete. The hierarchy is now fixed: NG + NEOGEN leads, the star supports compact and decorative surfaces.

Fix

The live page includes copy artifacts such as "Thank you for reading this post" and weak auto-translations. These should not appear in the brand voice.

03 / usage rules

Rules That Remove Ambiguity

These rules settle the conflicts across the v1.0 exports and make the identity easier to apply in WooCommerce.

Do

  • Use NG + NEOGEN as the default lockup on storefront headers and official documents.
  • Keep GEN and the G in electric blue #00D1FF in dark mode.
  • Use the 8-point star as a supporting symbol, not as a replacement for the name.
  • Pair Arabic display copy with operational metadata for the NeoGen tone.

Do Not

  • Do not let the star and NG act as two competing primary logos on the same surface.
  • Do not use the serif NEOGEN wordmark from the old Brand System export.
  • Do not use "The Next-Gen Generation" or auto-translated UI fragments.
  • Do not put product CTAs in low-contrast cyan outlines without a clear primary action.

09 / rollout checklist

Store Alignment

These are the changes I would make next on the live WordPress side after the brand kit is approved.

Visual

  • Increase header lockup presence on desktop and mobile.
  • Raise hero calligraphy contrast and prevent bottom cropping on desktop.
  • Use the NG monogram for PWA icons and favicon; keep the 8-point star as a secondary icon/backdrop.
  • Standardize product-card CTAs to one filled primary action.

Content

  • Remove orphan blog plugin text from the homepage.
  • Replace "man categories" with "Main categories" or Arabic equivalent.
  • Clean SEO schema organization name so it does not say an unrelated brand.
  • Replace "Next-gen tech generation" with a cleaner English tagline.
