07 // USAGE RULES

Do / Don't

// DO

  • Use on Warm Black (#050505) or Ice White (#F5F6FA) backgrounds only.
  • Maintain minimum clear space = 1× cap-height of the wordmark on all sides.
  • Favicon minimum size: 16×16 px. Smaller = render blur — use monogram only.
  • Always pair the EN wordmark with AR tagline in bilingual contexts.
  • Keep "GEN" in Electric Blue (#00D1FF). Never recolor the accent.
  • Use Major Mono Display ONLY for the "NG" monogram. Not for body or headings.

// DON'T

  • Don't rotate the wordmark or monogram.
  • Don't apply drop shadows, outlines, or gradient overlays to the marks.
  • Don't change the weight contrast — "NEO" stays 300, "GEN" stays 700.
  • Don't place on busy photos without a solid plate behind the mark.
  • Don't translate the wordmark into Arabic characters — "NEOGEN" is the trademark.
  • Don't use brass/gold tokens anywhere. That era is deprecated.
